About Our Rafting Adventures

Our History

Dry Oar Rafting Company was founded in 2001 with a mission to share the beauty and thrill of whitewater rafting with adventurers from around the world.

What began with just a couple of rafts and a passion for nature has grown into a team of experienced guides and thousands of happy customers. Our commitment to safety, sustainability, and unforgettable experiences continues to drive everything we do.
